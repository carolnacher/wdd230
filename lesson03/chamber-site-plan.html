Site Plan - Montevideo Chamber of Commerce

Carol Nacher

WDD 230

Purpose

The Montevideo Chamber of Commerce helps foster and advocate for the community. Promoting business through services for residents and tourists, seeking community economic development, being a leader in defending the general interest of commerce, services and the entire private business sector, as a driver of the economy

Scenarios

  • How can the Montevideo Chamber of Commerce help me identify export opportunities, provide information on international trade regulations and connect with potential business partners abroad?
  • How can the Montevideo Chamber of Commerce facilitate our establishment process in the country?
  • Do they provide information about the local business environment, regulations, and how to network with trusted business partners and suppliers in Montevideo?
  • Where can I find information about legal requirements, taxes and financing for startups in Montevideo?
